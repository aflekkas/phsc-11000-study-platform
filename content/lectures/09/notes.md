## High-Yield Content

- Absolute age estimates often rely on radioactive decay and known half-lives.
- Relative dating orders events without necessarily giving numeric ages.
- The geologic timescale organizes Earth history into eons, eras, periods, and smaller units.
- Major boundaries often correspond to biological turnover or environmental change.
- The final will likely reward knowing event order more than memorizing every date.
