## Role In The Course

Surveys post-Cambrian Paleozoic environmental/evolutionary events, emphasizing climate, sea level, paleogeography, radiations, and extinctions.

## Lecture Goals

- Know key environmental and evolutionary events in the post-Cambrian Paleozoic.
- Track climate, sea level, and paleogeography through Ordovician, Silurian, Devonian, Carboniferous, and Permian.
- Understand major Paleozoic radiations and mass extinctions.

## High-Yield Content

- Ordovician: high sea level and epicontinental seas support major marine diversification, then glaciation and extinction.
- Silurian: marine recovery, vascular plants, arthropods on land, changing oceans and orogenies.
- Devonian: fish radiation, land-plant diversification, tetrapods, reef communities, and Late Devonian extinction/anoxia.
- Carboniferous: coal swamps, crinoid gardens, high O2, carbon burial, amphibians and early reptiles.
- Permian: Pangaea, aridification, icehouse-to-greenhouse transition, gymnosperms/reptiles, setup for end-Permian crisis.
