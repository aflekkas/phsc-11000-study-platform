## Role In The Course

Shows how rock composition, sedimentary structures, and fossils reveal depositional environments.

## Lecture Goals

- Use rock composition as a clue to depositional environment.
- Use sedimentary structures as environmental evidence.
- Use fossil content to infer setting.

## High-Yield Content

- Sedimentary composition reflects source material, transport, chemistry, and biology.
- Sedimentary structures record energy, flow direction, water depth, exposure, and depositional processes.
- Fossil content can indicate salinity, depth, oxygenation, substrate, and environment.
- Environmental interpretation combines multiple clues, not a single feature.
