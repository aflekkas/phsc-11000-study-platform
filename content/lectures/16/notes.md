Source PDF: `source.pdf`

## Role In The Course

Uses a major climate controversy to connect evidence, feedbacks, and Earth-system recovery.

## Lecture Goals

- Understand the Cryogenian conundrum.
- Explain the Snowball Earth hypothesis.
- Know current controversies.

## High-Yield Content

- Snowball Earth proposes extreme low-latitude/global glaciation in the Cryogenian.
- Evidence includes glacial deposits at low paleolatitudes and unusual cap carbonates.
- Ice-albedo feedback can intensify cooling.
- Volcanic CO2 buildup is a proposed escape mechanism when weathering is suppressed.
- Debates involve how complete the ice cover was, ocean refugia, and biological survival.

## Study Targets

- Know evidence -> hypothesis -> controversy structure.
- Be able to explain runaway ice-albedo feedback and greenhouse escape.
- Connect to carbon cycle and oxygen/life transitions.

## Active Recall

- How could Earth escape a global glaciation?
