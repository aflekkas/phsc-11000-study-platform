## Prep Goal

The final should be studied as a connected Earth-history story, not as isolated lecture memorization:

- Modern environmental controls: energy budget, atmosphere, oceans, plate tectonics, organisms.
- Geological inference tools: rocks, stratigraphy, time, sedimentology, stable isotopes, paleogeography.
- Earth-system feedbacks: carbon cycle, climate, oxygen, sea level, tectonics, life.
- Major transitions: origin of life, oxygenation, Snowball Earth, Cambrian Radiation, terrestrialization, mass extinctions, Mesozoic Marine Revolution, mammals, humans.

Use the lecture map first, then build active-recall sheets from the weakest clusters.
