## Most Likely Compare/Contrast Pairs

- Cambrian Radiation vs. Mesozoic Marine Revolution.
- End-Ordovician vs. Late Devonian vs. End-Permian vs. End-Cretaceous extinctions.
- Paleozoic marine communities vs. post-Paleozoic marine communities.
- Oxygenation vs. Snowball Earth as Earth-system feedback events.
- Coal-swamp carbon burial vs. modern fossil carbon release.
- Natural climate shifts vs. industrial climate change.
- Land plants vs. humans as ecosystem engineers.

## Short-Answer Formula

For any event:

1. Name the event and place it in sequence.
2. State the mechanism.
3. Name the evidence.
4. State the consequence.
5. Connect it to one earlier or later lecture.
