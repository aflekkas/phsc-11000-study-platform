## High-Yield Clusters

- Climate controls: insolation, greenhouse gases, atmosphere, oceans, carbon cycle.
- Deep-time tools: stratigraphy, geologic time, sedimentology, stable isotopes, paleogeography.
- Earth-system change: plate tectonics, sea level, oxygen, carbon burial, climate feedbacks.
- Biological transitions: origin of life, cyanobacteria/photosynthesis, animals, land plants/animals, mammals, humans.
- Extinctions and recoveries: Paleozoic events, end-Permian, Mesozoic events, Cenozoic recovery and diversification.

## Practice Loop

For each cluster, answer these without notes:

1. What changed?
2. What caused it?
3. What evidence lets geologists infer it?
4. What feedbacks made it bigger, smaller, or longer-lasting?
5. How does it connect to at least one earlier and one later lecture?
