## How To Review Figures Efficiently

For each figure, do not ask "what does this show?" Ask:

1. What variable is changing?
2. What process causes the change?
3. What evidence is being plotted or illustrated?
4. What conclusion does the instructor want me to draw?
5. Which later lecture reuses this same process?
