# PHSC 11000 Syllabus Notes

Environmental History of the Earth, Spring 2026.

## Course Structure

- Lecture: Monday, Wednesday, Friday, 11:30 AM-12:20 PM, HGS 101.
- Lab: HGS 430.
- Instructor: Mark Webster.
- Textbook: none.

## Grading

- Final exam: 30%.
- Lab exercises: 50%, eight labs with worst score dropped.
- Lab quizzes: 20%, six quizzes with worst score dropped.
- Optional extra-credit term paper: up to 5%.

## Final Exam

- Regular final exam: Tuesday, May 26, 2026, 6:00 PM-8:00 PM for 11:30 MWF courses, per the UChicago Registrar final exam schedule.
- Course-details PDF also lists an early final for graduating seniors: Sunday, May 24, 2026, 2:00 PM-4:00 PM, HGS 301.
